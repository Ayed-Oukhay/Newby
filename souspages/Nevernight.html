In a land where three suns almost never set, a fledgling killer joins a school of assassins,

seeking vengeance against the powers who destroyed her family.Daughter of an executed traitor,

Mia Corvere is barely able to escape her father’s failed rebellion with her life. Alone and friendless,

she hides in a city built from the bones of a dead god, hunted by the Senate and her father’s former comrades.

Writer: JAY KRISTOFF
