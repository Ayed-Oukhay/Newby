Prosper is the only unexceptional Redding in his old and storied family history.

that is, until he discovers the demon living inside him. Turns out Prosper's

great-great-great-great-great-something grandfather made — and then broke — a contract with a malefactor,

a demon who exchanges fortune for eternal servitude. And, weirdly enough, four-thousand-year-old Alastor

isn't exactly the forgiving type.

Writer: ALEXANDRA BRACKEN
