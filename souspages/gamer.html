In a future mind-controlling game, death row convicts are forced to battle in a 'Doom'-type environment.

Convict Kable, controlled by Simon, a skilled teenage gamer, must survive thirty sessions in order to be set free.

Or won't he?

Director : Mark Neveldine , Brian Taylor

Writers : Mark Neveldine , Brian Taylor

Cast : Gerard Butler, Michael C. Hall, Ludacris...
